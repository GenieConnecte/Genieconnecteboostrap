Our Book

Speaking Architecture: From Concept to Construction to Completion

Our book Speaking Architecture starts with our belief that all buildings matter. It takes first account lessons of past projects and builds a guide for the intricate process of construction. It creates a holistic image of all that is involved in an architectural project, from defining and designing a project, to building it.
A guide book for prospective clients who want to understand the value of speaking the common language of architecture with design professionals. For professionals looking to understand the process from a different point of view. For anyone looking to understand the value of being a part of the architectural process.
